Michael and Anna

THE STORY OF US

We met online nad began seeing each other in the spring of 2019.

Our favorite thing is family and we spend a lot of time with them at picnics and gatherings.

Michael bought a house, his great-grandmother's home, a family residence, in 2000.

Having grown up with dogs the house felt empty; he got a puppy from a rescue group and named him Ruger.

Anna graduated from Ivy Tech with her AS in Business Administration in the spring of 2020.

Anna moved in toward the summer of 2020 and that fall they got another puppy, Winnie.

Michael graduated with his BS in Business Administration in the Spring of 2021 from the University of Louisville.

He was featured in the UofL's College of Business in March.

Michael proposed to Anna on April 3, 2021 at The Louisville Zoo during their Festival of Lights near the swans.

The crowd made way for the engagment to many excited cheers and applause.

We are looking forward to continuing to build this beautiful life!
